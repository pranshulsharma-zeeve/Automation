# Open Questions

All unresolved assumptions and ambiguities discovered while generating the Foundation Pack.

## Product & Users
1. What is the exact product domain and end-customer problem to solve?
2. Who is the primary persona for MVP (PM, EM, Designer, QA, or mixed)?
3. Is MVP internal-only or customer-facing?
4. What are measurable success metrics for MVP (cycle-time, defect rate, rework reduction)?

## Scope & Delivery
5. What is the target MVP launch date?
6. What explicit features are mandatory for day-1 release?
7. Which items must be deferred to post-MVP?

## Architecture & Platform
8. What runtime platform is required (web app only, API-only, or hybrid)?
9. Are there mandated cloud/vendor constraints?
10. What scale target is expected (projects, users, concurrent edits)?

## Security & Compliance
11. What authentication model is required (SSO, OAuth, local auth)?
12. Are there compliance obligations (SOC2, HIPAA, GDPR, data residency)?
13. What data classification and retention policies apply?

## Process & Governance
14. Who approves each artifact type before backlog generation?
15. What is the required review SLA for artifacts and task packages?
16. Are there required templates/standards from existing PMO or engineering governance?

## API & Integrations
17. Should API contracts support external integrations in MVP?
18. Which systems must integrate first (GitHub, Jira, Linear, Slack)?
19. Is webhook/eventing required in MVP or later phase?

## QA & Release
20. What is the minimum release quality bar (coverage thresholds, test types)?
21. Is a staging/UAT environment required for MVP?
22. What risk tolerance determines release/no-release decisions?
